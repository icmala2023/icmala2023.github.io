ICMA Music Showcase 2023: Latin America

The International Computer Music Association (ICMA) presents the ICMA Music Showcase 2023: Latin America. Members of the ICMA born or resident in Latin America are invited to submit pieces for a series of online concerts from 14 to 15 July, 2023. Fixed medium pieces must be in the categories of a) electroacoustic music; b) recordings of performances of electroacoustic music with instruments; and c) audiovisual works.

The event will be streamed on YouTube and will remain online for two weeks.

The submissions will be double-blind, peer reviewed, and the highest scoring works in each category will receive an honorary mention.

Requirements for submissions
Submissions will only be accepted from members of the ICMA. If you are not a member, you can register at this link before submitting: https://icma.wildapricot.org/Membership

Pieces must be from 2017 or later. They can be previously performed.

Submissions must:

  • a) Audio-only: provide a download link in either .wav or .aiff format
  • b) and c) A download link for audiovisual recordings (music with instruments or audiovisual works): 1080p (1920x1080) in .mov or .mp4 format.
Download links must remain available until 15 July 2023.

Submission site: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICMALA2023

Submissions will be blind reviewed by an international committee of experts and curated by the organisers. Selected works will be available online for two weeks after the event.

Streamed live on YouTube and Meia

Direct links to the live broadcasts made through the Youtube and Meia platforms will be available on this page.

It serves composers, engineers, researchers and musicians who are interested in the integration of music and technology.
